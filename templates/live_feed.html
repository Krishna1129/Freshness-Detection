Real‑time freshness monitor

Your default webcam is streaming into Freshness Cloud. Move crates or trays into the frame to inspect them before acceptance.

Tips for best live results

  • Use bright, even lighting to minimise shadows and glare.
  • Keep produce roughly 30–60cm from the camera for optimal framing.
  • Hold crates steady for a second while the system stabilises detections.
  • Avoid highly reflective packaging that may confuse the detector.

Session controls

Close this browser tab or stop the Python process running app.py to end the stream.
